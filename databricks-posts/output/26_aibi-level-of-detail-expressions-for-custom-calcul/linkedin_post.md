In the world of data visualization, clarity can get blurry real quick. But today's announcement from Databricks brings a breath of fresh air. Introducing Level of Detail Expressions (LOD), a game-changer for custom calculations that puts control back in our hands. Here's why it matters:

LOD expressions allow us to adjust the granularity of aggregation independently of visualization groupings. This means we can calculate percentages, compare values across the dataset, and create cohort-level metrics with ease. But here's the catch—we don't need to change our data model or write complicated SQL queries to do it.

So how does it work? Databricks provides two types of LOD expressions: fixed and coarser. Fixed expressions let us aggregate over specific dimensions, regardless of groupings in visualizations. Coarser expressions, on the other hand, allow us to aggregate at a higher level by excluding dimensions.

This feature is a testament to Databricks' commitment to making data analysis more efficient and intuitive for teams like ours.
